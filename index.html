About Me

I worked for about six years in the oilfields of Southern Texas. The work was dangerous and seldom rewarding. Before long I found myself looking for a career that would suit my mode of thinking. I've always been the type of person who relished the opportunity to solve problems and learn new things. After discovering web development, I decided that's what I needed to do.
